# PicckR


## What is PicckR?
PicckR is a delivery app that helps people Individuals  and businesses send and receive items faster, safer, and cheaper.  
It brings everything into one app — creating orders, paying, and tracking your package.


## Why It Matters
Most delivery apps are slow, expensive, and hard to track.  
PicckR solves that by making delivery simple, affordable, and transparent.


## Who It’s For
- Small businesses  
- Online sellers  
- Individuals sending parcels  
- Riders who want to earn fairly  


## Key Features
- Onboarding and Account Creation
- Single Order delivery
- Multi-stop delivery (one order, many drop-offs)  
- Admin dashboard feature for the Admins to track orders and metrics
- Real-time tracking and Notifications  
- Verification for drivers(driving license, NiN, Road worthiness Certificate)
- Availability & Route management(save preferred routes, Go offline/online )
- Rider bidding (fair delivery prices)  
- Wallet and Earnings (payments and refunds)
- Account Recovery(Password reset)  
- Ratings and reviews  


## Goals
- Reduce delivery delays by 80%  
- Reach ₦50 million in transactions  
- Grow users by 20% every quarter
